Admission Requirement

The access to the Two Year technical Degree is made on « post-bac » ( only for French students ) , admittance reports, interview, even tests ; Mostly science degree, and also Sciences and technologies of the industry and the sustainable development degree with speciality Information system and Digital technology or still economic and social sciences degree, or Sciences and technologies of the management with speciality management information system.

In special year (the duration of training is of one year), it is necessary to have validated 60 European credits or followed a higher education of 2 years and to pass in front of a jury of admission.

Registration

For French Students : Any request of candidacy make exclusively via the site « post-bac admission »

It is necessary to make a letter in support of application and a curriculum vitae.

To anticipate

Have some bases in programmins is not necessarily but advised for an ease of learning. Have a suitable level in Enlgish : test of English to establish the groups of TD at the start of the school year.
