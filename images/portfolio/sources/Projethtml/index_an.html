Department IT Metz

The current technical evolution, favors the access to the computer systems.
The essential objective of this DUT is to form future IT specialists who will be capable of designing, of realizing, of operating and of maintaining the computer systems corresponding to needs formulated by the users.

Courses are mainly 25 students in T.D (tutorial class), 12 students in T.P (practical classe) and also the presence of lectures. This, are in full class.
The diploma gets ready in 2 years (4 half-years). It's also possible in 1 year by a special year for the wealthy students already have a level bac+2.

The IUT offers the advantages of the university, with a supplement of supervision.
